• Martin Grim

    Section Head Electronics Engineering

    The Engineering Group (EG) at SRON designs and builds advanced research and aerospace instruments in collaboration with scientists and external partners. The team of 55–60 engineers supports most SRON projects, including satellite, aircraft, and balloon instruments, and is divided into four disciplines: Electronic Design, Mechanical Design, Product & Quality Assurance, and Engineering Realization.

  • Benjamin Ricketts

    PhD candidate

    I work on numerical models for X-ray astronomy, focusing on the reltrans model that simulates X-ray emission near black holes. To handle its complexity, I use machine learning to emulate model outputs, speeding up data analysis and improving the code. I also serve as Principal Investigator for the NEBULA-Xplorer mission at SRON, an educational satellite project where students design and build a real mission to monitor X-ray binary systems and study phenomena such as jet ejections and accretion processes.
